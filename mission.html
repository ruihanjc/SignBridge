Estimated 5% of the world's population, or around 430 million people, suffer from deaf or hard of hearing. Among this group, a significant percentage uses sign language as their primary means of communication. However, not everyone understands or can communicate through sign language. This creates a communication barrier, limiting the deaf community's social profile and the ability to study and to get employed.only 34% of UK’s deaf population are able to complete 2 A-Levels or equivalent. This has led to more than 50% illiteracy rate for the deaf community.
